The Palo Alto hospital (1959) in Palo Alto, California. The architect is [Edward Durell Stone](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_Durell_Stone) also known for the [Kennedy Center in Washington, D.C.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_F._Kennedy_Center_for_the_Performing_Arts#/media/File:Kennedy_Center_seen_from_the_Potomac_River,_June_2010.jpg) and the [US embassy in New Delhi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Embassy_of_the_United_States,_New_Delhi#/media/File:US_Embassy_New_Delhi.jpg).
