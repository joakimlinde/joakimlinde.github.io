The [Edwards building](https://campus-map.stanford.edu/?id=07-309&lat=37.43268048&lng=-122.17623231&zoom=17) (1959) at Stanford hospital in Palo Alto, California. The architect is [Edward Durell Stone](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_Durell_Stone) also known for MoMA in NYC and the US embassy in New Delhi. 

The actual color of the building is beige but the trees gives it a green colorcast that I found interesting and modern. I decided to keep a hint of the colorcast but reduce it significantly — my apologies to Mr. Stone.
