The [James H. Clark Center](https://biox.stanford.edu/about/clark-center), home to 
[Stanford Bio-X](https://biox.stanford.edu), at Stanford University. Designed by 
[Foster+Partners](https://www.fosterandpartners.com) in collaboration with 
MBT Architecture (later acquired by [Perkins&amp;Will](https://perkinswill.com)). 
Construction began in 2001 and it opened in 2003.
