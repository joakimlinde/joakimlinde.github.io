Once upon a time this was the Varsity theater in Palo Alto, California. It has been turned into a new nice public space — a [Blue Bottle cafe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blue_Bottle_Coffee). There is also a co-working space — [HanaHaus](https://www.hanahaus.com) — in the back.
