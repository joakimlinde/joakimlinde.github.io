# About

I’m a photographer based in Palo Alto, California who focus on photographing urban 
environments. Some call it architectural photography, but I prefer the term urban 
since it includes more than just the architecture.

I have a <a href="https://joakimlinde.photography">portfolio site</a>, please check
it out. You can also follow or contact me though social media or send me message below.
